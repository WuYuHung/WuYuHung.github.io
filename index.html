About Me

吳昱宏 Yu-Hung (Edward) Wu

Hello! I'm Edward Wu, currently a Master's student at the Graduate Institute of Communication Engineering, National Taiwan University, under the guidance of Prof. Shao-Hua Sun. I have a strong academic background and extensive experience in machine learning, with specific research interests in Large Language Models (LLMs) and Reinforcement Learning (RL).

Previously, I worked as a research assistant at Academia Sinica, where I contributed to the CKIP Lab. This lab focuses on building research infrastructures with reusable resources for natural language processing (NLP).
